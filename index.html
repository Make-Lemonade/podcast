Lemonaid +

At the core of Make Lemonade, everything we do is centered around helping creators earn money. The Make Lemonade show will look to not only inspire other creators to make a living doing what they love, but we’re also putting our money where our mouth is. That’s why we’re excited to announce the Lemonaid + Creator Fund.

Lemonaid + aims to give back to the creators that are building inspiring products with inspiring stories. Each month, creators can apply to be the recipient of the fund. The monthly winner of the fund will receive cash (could even be 1% of sales), Lemon Squeezy PRO annual account, Iconic PRO annual account, and a remote work start pack (Rode mic, Audio Technica headphones, and a Lumina AI powered webcam.)
